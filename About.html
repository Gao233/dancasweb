About Dancas:

Dancas Dance club was estabablished in 2011 or sometime there.

Dancas is a club in the University Of Wisconsin-Madison. We focus on hiphop culture and also have classic Chinese dance or modern dance. Dance is our attitude so keep on dancing! We are always willing to welcome new blood like you to join our family and make dancas more active and fun.

Dancas是美国威斯康辛麦迪逊大学舞社，社团活动以街舞为主，兼辅古典民族现代舞等。用舞蹈表达我们共同的情感和追求，KEEP ON DANCING！欢迎有兴趣或功底的同学加入我们，也欢迎联系演出比赛等活动！
